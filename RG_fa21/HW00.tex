\begin{homeworkProblem}
    \pagebreak

    \part

    Calculate the bias and the variance for the estimated slope
    \(\hat{\beta_1}\).
    \\

    \solution

    For the bias, we need to calculate the expected value
    \(\E[\hat{\beta_1}]\):

    \[
        \begin{split}
            \E[\hat{\beta_1}]
            &= \E \left[ \frac{
                \sum {x_i Y_i}
            }{
                \sum x_i^2
            }\right]
            \\
            &= \frac{
                \sum {x_i \E[Y_i]}
            }{
                \sum x_i^2
            }
            \\
            &= \frac{
                \sum {x_i (\beta_1 x_i)}
            }{
                \sum x_i^2
            }
            \\
            &= \frac{
                \sum {x_i^2 \beta_1}
            }{
                \sum x_i^2
            }
            \\
            &= \beta_1 \frac{
                \sum {x_i^2 \beta_1}
            }{
                \sum x_i^2
            }
            \\
            &= \beta_1
        \end{split}
    \]

    Thus since our estimator's expected value is \(\beta_1\), we can conclude
    that the bias of our estimator is 0.
    \\

    For the variance:

    \[
        \begin{split}
            \Var[\hat{\beta_1}]
            &= \Var \left[ \frac{
                \sum {x_i Y_i}
            }{
                \sum x_i^2
            }\right]
            \\
            &=
            \frac{
                \sum {x_i^2}
            }{
                \sum x_i^2 \sum x_i^2
            } \Var[Y_i]
            \\
            &=
            \frac{
                \sum {x_i^2}
            }{
                \sum x_i^2 \sum x_i^2
            } \Var[Y_i]
            \\
            &=
            \frac{
                1
            }{
                \sum x_i^2
            } \Var[Y_i]
            \\
            &=
            \frac{
                1
            }{
                \sum x_i^2
            } \sigma^2
            \\
            &=
            \frac{
                \sigma^2
            }{
                \sum x_i^2
            }
        \end{split}
    \]

\end{homeworkProblem}
